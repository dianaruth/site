About Me

Hello! My name is Diana Ruth and I'm a fifth year student at The University of Texas at Austin. I am a double major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science graduating in May 2017. I love everything about my majors and I'm incredibly excited to make the move to full-time work this summer. During my time at UT, I was a member of Theta Tau, a national co-ed professional engineering fraternity, and I was the president of the Texas Equestrian Team. In my free time, you can find me knitting, biking, cooking, or spending time at the barn.

Since I'm studying both electrical engineering and computer science, I've been exposed to a large variety of concepts that has provided me with a well-rounded understanding of software engineering. I've taken classes on circuits, logic design, operating systems and more, but my true passion lies in web design and development. I love learning new languages and frameworks in my spare time to further develop my software development skills, and I'm hoping to pursue a career that allows me use the newest technologies to solve all types of problems.
