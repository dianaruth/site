//About//

Hello! My name is Diana Ruth and I'm a fourth year student at The University of Texas at Austin. I am a double major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science set to graduate in May of 2017 and I'm absolutely loving it. I'm a member of Theta Tau, a national co-ed professional engineering fraternity, and I'm the president of the Texas Equestrian Team. In my free time, you can find me running, biking, cooking, or spending time at the barn.

Since I'm studying both electrical engineering and computer science, I've been exposed to a large variety of concepts that has provided me with a well-rounded understanding of software engineering. I've taken classes on circuits, logic design, operating systems and more, but nothing gets me as excited as web development does. I have an intense passion for designing and implementing web sites and I would love to work with web and mobile development when I graduate.
